My approach:

  • is person-centred, beginning with you, however you are on the day;
  • is systemic in seeing both internal and external worlds as networks of situations, processes and relationships;
  • is cognitive-behavioural in working with thoughts, feelings and behaviours as the core elements of subjective experience;
  • is analytic in working with dialogue and an attitude of exploration;
  • is Jungian in appreciating images and metaphors, and being curious about dreams.

About me

My background is in design, technology and music, working as a team lead, mentor and teacher. Design work is essentially about asking questions and exploring possibilities. I always found my work with people to be the most rewarding, whether helping clients to articulate a vision, or helping colleagues to manage stress and uncertainty. My work led me to psychology, philosophy and personal development. Through journalling I became interested in my dreams, which led me to the work of C.G. Jung, and in turn to an interest in psychoanalysis, and depth psychology in general.
